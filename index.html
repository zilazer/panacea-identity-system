02 / Design Language

Less typography, more symbol.

The wordmark is an original geometric lettering system: uniform stroke, 45-degree chamfer, modular construction, open geometry.

字标不是普通字体，而是一套原创几何字母系统：统一线宽、45 度切角、模块化构成、开放式几何。

Uniform stroke / 统一线宽

Every mark should feel constructed from the same industrial logic. 所有标识应像从同一套工业逻辑中构造出来。

45-degree chamfer / 45 度切角

The chamfer is the system's recurring architectural detail. 切角是系统中反复出现的建筑化细节。

Open geometry / 开放几何

Open counters and folded forms make the mark read as a symbol. 开放字腔与折线结构让字标更接近符号。
